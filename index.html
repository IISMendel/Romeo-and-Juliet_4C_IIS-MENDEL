ENGLISH

Though set in Verona, the play reflects the anxieties of England in 1596. Queen Elizabeth I ruled a religiously divided country. Her father, Henry VIII, created the Anglican schism, causing conflicts with Catholics. The Montague-Capulet feud mirrors the English fear of Civil War: ideological hatred destroying the nation.
